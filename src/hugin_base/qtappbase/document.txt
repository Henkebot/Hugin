* division of data and document *
Lower level and higher level functionalities; higher one maybe supplied by the GUI framework.
eg. wxPanoramaDocument should inherit from wxDocument and Panorama (data) class.

 data: data read/write to iostream (in AppBase)
 document: works as the controller, handles the document location, file type etc. (in widgets dependent layer)
